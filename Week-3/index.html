Response:
Access Intimacy: The Missing Link

I really enjoyed reading this article because it portrayed the issue of accessibility and disability justice in such a tangible, understandable way. The way that Mia Mingus describes the feeling of access intimacy feels like something I've experienced before, in various situations, but applied to other situations, many of which I haven't experienced. It makes me care more about designing items that can provide people with a sense of access intimacy because I understand not feeling comfortable, and i've felt the joy of feeling taken care of.
