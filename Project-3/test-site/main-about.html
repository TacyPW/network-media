About Us

The "Target is for Everyone" project was born out of our desire to create a shopping experience that truly reflects the diverse world we live in. We believe that every individual deserves to be celebrated and included, and that's exactly what this initiative represents.

Through our inclusive practices and product offerings, we aim to break down barriers, challenge norms, and promote a sense of belonging for transgender and nonbinary individuals. By embracing and respecting everyone's unique identities, we hope to create a world where diversity is celebrated and inclusivity is the norm.
